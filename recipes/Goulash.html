Goulash Recipe

Description

This dish brings me back to my childhood. It consists of ground beef, macaroni noodles, and ketchup... YUM!

Ingredients

    1 lb Ground Beef
    1 lb Macaroni noodles
    Ketchup

Steps

  1. Boil water and dump in noodles
  2. Boil until aldente
  3. Remove noodles
  4. Cook ground beef on skillet for 5-8 minutes
  5. Combine noodles and ground beef into a bowl
  6. Top with ketchup and ENJOY!
