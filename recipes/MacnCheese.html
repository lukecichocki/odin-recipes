Mac n Cheese Recipe

Description

Enjoy this Mac n Cheese recipe that tastes like grandma's family famous recipe!

Ingredients

    1 lb of Elbow Macaroni
    3 lbs of shredded Cheddar cheese
    Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil and throw in noodles
  2. Boil until aldente
  3. Remove noodles and strain
  4. Add noodles to a mixing bowl, and sprinkle in cheese
  5. Add desired amount of salt and pepper
  6. Move Mac n Cheese into a serving bowl, and ENJOY!
