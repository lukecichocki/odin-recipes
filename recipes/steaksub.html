Steak Sub Recipe

Description

A Pete Cy's classic sub smothered in Boss Sauce & Swiss American Cheese. Try one for yourself today!

Ingredients

    2.5 lbs of Shaved Sorlein Steak
    1 pk 12 count Costanzo Sub Roll
    1 lb of Swiss American Cheese
    1 jar of Mild or Hot Boss Sauce

Steps

  1. Heat skillet to medium high
  2. Chop steak meat on the hot skillet
  3. Once cooked, apply Boss Sauce & cheese on top of the steak
  4. Toast roll in the oven for ~5 minutes
  5. Put meat on the roll, with mayonaisse
  6. Finally, ENJOY!
